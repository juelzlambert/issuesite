Highlighting Victories in the Battle Against Climate Change

In an era when discussions about climate change frequently center on problems and crises, it is important to recognize the incredible progress and successes in our collective battle against this worldwide concern.

An impressive triumph in the battle against climate change is the rapid expansion of renewable energy sources across nations within the U.N.

Nations all over the world are quickly moving toward hydroelectric, wind, and solar energy. Notably, a few nations have achieved remarkable records for the number of days they have run entirely on renewable energy, indicating a positive move away from fossil fuels. In fact, according to sweden.se.com, in 2022, more than 60% of all of Sweden's electricity came from renewable sources.

Numerous countries and industries have pledged to reduce their carbon impact.

Many significant economies have established ambitious objectives to become carbon neutral in the next several decades. Sweden, for example, plans to be using 100% renewable energy by 2040 and they are on track to reach this goal!

Thankfully, there has been a rise in environmental activism as individuals band together to demand laws and policies that put the environment first. Global demonstrations, youth-led projects, and extensive awareness campaigns are pressuring businesses, governments, and consumers to take significant action.

Despite the crisis, many nations are taking steps in a good direction!

Julia Lambert 2023
